BWSS ARMAP PRIVACY STATEMENT

Your privacy is important to Bitwise South Stadium Augmented Reailty Map creator: Csaba Toth. This privacy statement provides information about the information that BWSS ARMap collects.

Personal information collection

BWSS ARMap does not collect any kind of information information, no personal information whatsoever

Using information

The camera permission is needed because in the beginning the Augmented Reality engine (Sceneform) scans the scene to find the activator image pattern. That pattern will activate the placement of the augmented class room signs. On top of that to provide the actual reality part of the augmented reality experience the camera stream is needed as well. BWSS ARMap does not record, save or transfer any of the camera, bluetooth or any data whatsoever. The app doesn't even contain any advertisement framework, and does not require any authentication either.

Securing data, transfers

Since no data is collected, there's no way to secure non existing data, and there's nothing to transfer either.

Updating this statement

BWSS ARMap may update this privacy policy by posting a new version on this website.

You should check this page occasionally to ensure you are familiar with any changes.

Other frameworks

BWSS ARMap is not responsible for the privacy policies or practices of any third party. This means that if internals of Android operating system, Sceneform or ARCore, or any implicitly provided frameworks by the Android eco-system would record anything, that's outside of the jurisdiction of BWSS ARMap application creator.

Contact BWSS ARMap

If you have any questions about this privacy policy or BWSS ARMap treatment of your personal informationyou can contact the creator on LinkedIn
